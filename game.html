How to Play

Your goal is to match the target color by adjusting the Red, Green, and Blue sliders.

  • Use the RGB sliders to change your color
  • Click "Check Match" to see how close you are
  • Lower difference means better match (perfect = 0)
  • Get a perfect match to earn 10 points
  • Get a close match (difference < 30) to earn 5 points
  • Click "New Color" for a new target color
